Q: What is Crosswalk guards?
A: Crosswalk guards direct the movement of children along or
across highways going to or from school. They signal drivers
to stop by holding up a stop sign (paddle) facing the vehicle.
Drivers must obey crossing guards appointed and employed
for this purpose. Failure to do so could mean demerit points
on your driving record.

Q: What to do when approached by an emergency vehicle?
A: As the emergency vehicle approaches from ahead or behind, move to the right edge or curb of the road and stop completely.
Remain stopped until the emergency vehicle has passed, or until you are directed to move
by a peace officer.
